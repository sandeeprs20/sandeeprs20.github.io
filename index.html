About Me

I am Sandeep Sreenivasan, a high school senior at National Hill View Public School, Bangalore, India. I enjoy using AI and other technology to help solve modern social problems existing in community.
I have taken part in many robot building competitions and have attended courses in AI and Web Developement.

In addition to this, I blog on the various community problems and technology trends, have written research papers on various topics and have coded various robotic models out of passion.
