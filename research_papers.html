About my Internship

I interned as a software intern in the IT company, Envestnet Yodlee, A leading data aggregation and data analytics platform, which help consumers live better financial lives through innovative products and services created for financial institutions and FinTech companies. I was assigned work under the data analytics team. Data analytics was a subject I knew very little about so I was ready to learn about it.

In the internship, I was given data of about 2000 account holders who payed off their loans in a lumpsum. I was required to predict the behaviour of the users as to what lead to them paying off their loans in the form of a python code. This internship helped me in a lot of ways, mainly broadening my vision about the subject of computer science.
