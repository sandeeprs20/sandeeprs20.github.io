Community Drive

Wanting to promote the idea of sustainability and reusability, I started an initiative, ‘Responsible Recology’, to collect laptops, refurbish, and donate them to a charitable organisation, Shastri's school for the dumb and deaf. This way, e-waste accumulation in landfills could drastically be reduced and the devices could be used as an effective source of education for the underprivileged. I donated 7 laptops and a printer to the Shastri's School of Dumb and Deaf.

As a result of my community service, I learnt about giving back to society. Through this process, I have not only ensured that I sustainably reused the gadgets but also reduced the e-waste that was being generated. Seeing the joy on the faces of the children in the 'Shastri' charitable organisation inspired me to help instil flexibility to as many people as I could by continuing the drive.
